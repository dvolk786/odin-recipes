Vegan Brownie

Description

The recipe for Vegan & Gluten-Free Fudgy Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Brownies is a delightful treat for those with dietary restrictions or anyone who loves a rich and decadent dessert. These brownies are flourless, vegan, and gluten-free, making them suitable for a variety of diets. They are made with natural peanut butter, unsweetened applesauce, cocoa powder, and honey (or a vegan alternative). The result is a fudgy, chocolatey delight with the added flavor of peanut butter and chocolate chips. These brownies are a perfect indulgence for anyone looking for a delicious and healthier sweet treat option.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup all natural peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup honey (agave or maple syrup if vegan)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 cup all natural unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt, if desired
  • 1/4 cup mini chocolate chips, plus 2 tablespoons for sprinkling on top

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Spray 8x8 inch pan with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. In a large bowl of electric mixer or food processor, cream together peanut butter, honey, vanilla, and applesauce until smooth.
  3. Add cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt; mix again until smooth.
  4. Gently fold 1/4 cup of chocolate chips into batter.
  5. Pour batter into prepared baking pan and smooth top.
  6. Bake for 18-22 minutes or until top is just set. It's okay if they look a little underdone; we want these to be fudgy! Plus, they are vegan so you don't need to worry about them cooking all the way through.
  7. Cool on wire rack as long as you can. Cut into 12 delicious squares!
